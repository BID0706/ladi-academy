LADI Academy

MSMEs constitute a vital sector of the Nigerian economy with a huge potential to drive sustainable development. Strengthen your commercial viability and development impact through our business development services that are critical to future growth and profitability.

LADI Academy is a subsidiary of LADI Business Development Services Limited, a private limited company, registered at the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja on 29th November 2019 with registration number RC 1635508. LADI Business Development Services Limited is an affiliate of Link Africa Diaspora Initiative ⟨LADI⟩, a private Limited company registered in England and Wales on 20th November 2017 with company number 11071130.

Our Mission

LADI Academy is an online membership platform to provide business development services to micro, small and medium enterprises ⟨MSMEs⟩ in Africa, commencing with the Nigerian market.

The LADI Academy seeks to empower MSMEs to strengthen their commercial viability and development impact by offering business development services that are critical to their future growth and profitability.
